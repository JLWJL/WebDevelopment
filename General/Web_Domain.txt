1. Hosting company: Most provide free domain registration. But may pay extra money for cancelling hosting contract and move the domain.

2. Registra: May also provide hosting service and easy to transfer domain to different hosting company.

3. WHOIS guard: protect your privacy when your domain is using WHOIS on your domain.
